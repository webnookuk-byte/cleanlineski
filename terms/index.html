3. Booking & Payment

Submitting an enquiry via our contact form does not constitute a booking. No payment is taken and no commitment is entered into at the enquiry stage.

A booking is confirmed only when:

  • Clean Line Ski has confirmed your place in writing; and

The balance of the trip price is due no later than 8 weeks before the trip departure date. If the balance is not received by this date, Clean Line Ski reserves the right to cancel your booking and retain the deposit.

For bookings made within 8 weeks of departure, full payment is required at the time of confirmation.

Cancellation by the guest:

All cancellations must be made in writing to info@cleanlineski.co.uk. Cancellation takes effect from the date the written notification is received.
